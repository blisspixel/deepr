# NNNN. Short title

- Status: Proposed | Accepted | Superseded by NNNN
- Date: YYYY-MM-DD

## Context

What problem forced a decision? What constraints and facts were in play?
Keep it to what a future reader needs to understand the choice.

## Decision

What we chose, stated plainly.

## Alternatives considered

The other real options, and why each was not chosen. (This is the part that
saves a future session from re-deriving the dead ends.)

## Consequences

What this makes easy, what it makes harder, and any follow-on work it
implies. Note explicitly what is now out of scope as a result.
